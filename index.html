About the Trust

Gosain Educational Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization founded with the vision to empower youth through education. Since its inception in 2010, the trust has actively provided scholarships, educational resources, and skill-building programs across Odisha. With a mission to create equal learning opportunities, we collaborate with schools, local bodies, and volunteers.
